For My Beautiful Sumu

Every flower blooms beautifully... But every time I see you, I realize that flowers only imitate a tiny part of your beauty.

Lily is elegant. Rajanigandha is fragrant. Rose is charming. Yet none of them carries your smile, your kindness, or the warmth that lives inside your heart.

Whenever the wind touches these flowers, I imagine it carrying my love towards you. Every petal whispers your name... Sumu.
